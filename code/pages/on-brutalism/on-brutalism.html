On Brutalism

A new trend emerged in web design the last year — Brutalist websites 1.

It is fueled by New Aesthetics 2 and it is for the new generation:

In its ruggedness and lack of concern to look comfortable or easy, Brutalism can be seen as a reaction by a younger generation to the lightness, optimism, and frivolity of today's webdesign.

Yes, we are fed up with the mainstream web, not just by it's look but by it's practices, tools and processes.

We are aware behind the "Same Looking Shiny UI"™ there is the same "Make The World Better"© bullshit marching exclusively for profit while destroying the basic principles of the web and the internet — us.
